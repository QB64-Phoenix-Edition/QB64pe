{{DISPLAYTITLE:_glBegin}}
The '''_glBegin''' and '''_glEnd''' statements delimit the vertices of a primitive or a group of like primitives.


{{PageSyntax}}
: '''_glBegin''' GLenum {{Parameter|mode}}
