{{DISPLAYTITLE:_glBindTexture}}
The '''_glBindTexture''' statement enables the creation of a named texture that is bound to a texture target.


{{PageSyntax}}
: '''_glBindTexture''' GLenum {{Parameter|target}}, GLuint {{Parameter|texture}}
